jsTetris V1.0 Alpha 


        Controls

        Menu:
      'W' ------ Up
      'S' ------ Down
      'Enter' -- Select

        Game:
      'A' ------ Move Left
      'D' ------ Move Right
      'Q' ------ Rotate Left
      'E' ------ Rotate Right
      'S' ------ Move Down
      'F5' ----- Restart
      'Enter' -- Pause Game
